California Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The California Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the state-wide organization dedicated to promoting business development and economic growth throughout California.

Site Purpose

The California Chamber of Commerce site aims to:

  • Promote local businesses through networking events and resources.
  • Advocate for business-friendly policies at the state and local levels.
  • Provide educational resources and training for businesses.

Target Audience

The target audience for the California Chamber of Commerce site includes:

  • Small and medium-sized business owners
  • Entrepreneurs and startups
  • Local government officials
  • Investors and business partners

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #d3d3d3d (used for headings and buttons)

Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (used for backgrounds and text)

Typography

Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif

Body Font: Georgia, serif
